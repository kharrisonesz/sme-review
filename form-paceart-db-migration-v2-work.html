PaceArt IT Administrator

Standard Migration — 7-Year Timeframe

The following data will be migrated from PaceArt to PaceMateLIVE™:

Patient Information
  • Demographics: First Name, Last Name, Middle Initial, DOB, Gender, Marital Status
  • Contact Information: Email, Address, Phone Numbers
  • Identifiers: Patient ID (Primary)
  • Notes: Patient Comments, Private Patient Comments
Encounters
  • Encounter Types: In-Clinic and Remote encounter types only
  • Clinical Data: Underlying Rhythm, Dependency (last known), Encounter Summary
  • Device Programming: Programming Mode, Device Battery Longevity
  • Device Information: PDF Encounter Attachments, Device (from Encounter Info)
Device Details
  • Device Information: Manufacturer, Model, Serial Number, Implant Date, Implanting Provider
  • Status: Active or explanted devices from associated encounters
  • Active Leads: Manufacturer, Model, Serial Number, Chamber, Implant Date
  • Device Advisories: Mitigation status and details (devices matching PaceMate® advisory registry are automatically flagged)
  • Deactivated Leads: Manufacturer, Model, Serial Number, Chamber, Implant Date (includes abandoned, partially explanted, and fully explanted leads)
Data Exclusions (Not Included in Standard Migration)
  • Patients with no encounters within the selected timeframe
  • Encounters dated outside the 7-year window
  • Additional exclusions as applicable
