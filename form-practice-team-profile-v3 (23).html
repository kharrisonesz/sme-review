Practice & Team Profile
Map your locations, team members, and roles for PaceMateLIVE™ configuration
Purpose
This form captures your complete practice structure — every location where cardiac device monitoring takes place and every team member who will interact with PaceMateLIVE™. PaceMate® uses this to configure your platform, provision user accounts, and plan your go-live.
How to Complete
Step 1: Enter your practice name and primary clinic details.
Step 2: Add every team member at that location — physicians, APPs, device nurses, admins, billers, and anyone who will use the system.
Step 3: If you have satellite locations, click + Add Satellite Location and repeat.
Step 4: Review the auto-generated practice map at the bottom to confirm your structure looks right.
